What I offer

Here at Heart Within Yoga, I believe yoga should be for everyone.

I specialise in Therapeutic Yoga for children and young adults with special needs.

I work with schools, care settings and private clients.

In my Therapeutic Yoga sessions, I use sensory and visual aids and tools, modelling poses, social stories and games. I make adaptations and modifications based on age and cognitive, social and emotional abilities.

I also teach Makaton signs yoga for nonverbal students.
